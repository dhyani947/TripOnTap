TripOnTap

TripOnTap Travels started as a small passion project by a group of students who loved exploring new places on a budget. We realised that many people want to travel but feel confused about planning, costs, and safety.

Our goal is simple: make travel easy, transparent and fun, especially for young people and first-time travellers.

What we care about

  • Clear Itineraries with realistic timings
  • Honest pricing with no surprise charges
  • Verified stays and trusted local partners
  • Experiences that respect nature and local culture
